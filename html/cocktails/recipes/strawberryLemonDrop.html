Strawberry Lemon Drop Martini

Making cocktails is fun! Drink responsibly and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Vodka(citrus) 60 ml
  • Lemon juice 30 ml
  • 3 Strawberries
  • 2 Bar spoons of fine sugar

Preparation

  1. Put the strawberries and the sugar in the shaker and muddle.
  2. Add other ingredients.
  3. Shake over ice and double strain into the glass.
  4. Garnish with a sugar rim, a strawberry and a lemon wheel.

Comment

This is a twist on the lemon drop martini, the drink tastes sweet rather than strong, make sure to use good strawberries.
