Alpha

Making cocktails is fun! Drink responsibly and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Campari 2 cl
  • Tequila blancho 2 cl
  • Agave syrup 1.5 cl
  • Lemon juice 2 cl
  • Orgeat syrup 1 dash
  • 8 raspberries

Preparation

  1. Put the raspberries in the shaker and muddle.
  2. Add other ingredients.
  3. Shake over ice.
  4. Double strain and pour into a chilled glass.
  5. Add garnish and serve.

Comment

This drink is delicious; it's really sweet, but you can still taste the sour of the raspberry and the just the right amount of bitterness from the Campari.

Double strain the mashed raspberries could take a while, and it makes your shaker and strainer pretty messy, so be aware. However, this is a great drink overall.
