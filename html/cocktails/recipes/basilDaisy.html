Basil Daisy

Making cocktails is fun! Drink responsibly and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Vodka 60 ml
  • Aperol 15 ml
  • Simple syrup 30 ml
  • Lemon juice 30 ml
  • Basil leaves

Instruction

  1. Tear 3 fresh basil leaves into half and put them into a shaker.
  2. Add other ingredients.
  3. Shake over ice and double strain into a chilled glass(with or without ice/crashed ice).
  4. Add garnish and serve.

Taste note

This lovely drink is sweet and full of the mixed taste of the bitterness of the Aperol and the herbal flavor from the basil. It doesn't taste strong, but it contains 60 ml of vodka, so don't drink too fast or too much. : )
